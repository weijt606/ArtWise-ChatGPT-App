Last updated: May 31, 2026

ArtWise is a lightweight museum exploration guide provided as a prototype ChatGPT App. By using ArtWise, you agree to these terms.

## Informational Use

ArtWise provides educational and travel-oriented museum guidance. Its artwork summaries, comparisons, image cards, map search terms, and suggested trails are informational only.

## Accuracy And Availability

ArtWise may not be able to confirm an artwork's exact title, artist, date, medium, current display status, or room location. Museum exhibitions and gallery layouts can change. Always verify important details using the museum wall label, official museum map, or museum staff.

ArtWise does not provide live museum collection access, real-time indoor navigation, ticketing, reservations, or accessibility routing.

## Comparison Images

ArtWise may display public artwork images hosted by Wikimedia Commons as visual comparison aids. A displayed comparison image does not prove that an artwork is currently on view or located nearby.

## User Responsibilities

Users should follow museum photography rules, visitor policies, and applicable laws. Do not upload content that you do not have permission to share.
