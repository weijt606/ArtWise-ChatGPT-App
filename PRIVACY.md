# ArtWise Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

ArtWise is a lightweight ChatGPT App that helps museum visitors turn one artwork photo or artwork description into a guided museum trail.

## Information ArtWise Receives

ArtWise may receive information that a user chooses to provide in ChatGPT, including:

- An artwork photo shared in the ChatGPT conversation.
- Museum, gallery, or room context provided by the user.
- Artwork title, artist name, description, or wall-label details provided by the user.
- User preferences such as interest in the artist, historical context, technique, themes, or nearby comparisons.

ArtWise does not require account creation, authentication, payment information, contact details, or precise location data.

## How ArtWise Uses Information

ArtWise uses the provided artwork context only to generate:

- A concise explanation of the current artwork.
- A confidence note when the artwork identity is uncertain.
- Related artwork comparison cards.
- A suggested mini trail through related artists, movements, periods, themes, or visual motifs.
- Cautious location guidance and map search terms.

ArtWise does not claim live access to museum collection systems, real-time room maps, or indoor navigation.

## Data Storage

This hack-night version of ArtWise does not use a database and does not create user accounts. The app server computes a response from the information provided in the current request.

Conversation data may still be processed and retained by ChatGPT/OpenAI according to the user's ChatGPT settings and OpenAI policies.

## Third-Party Resources

ArtWise may display public artwork comparison images hosted on Wikimedia Commons. These images are used as visual comparison aids. They do not prove that a work is currently on view or located nearby.

## Accuracy

ArtWise may be uncertain when an artwork photo, title, artist, room, or museum context is incomplete. Users should verify title, artist, date, medium, and location using the museum wall label or current museum map.
